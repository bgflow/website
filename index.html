About Me

Hey there! My name is Ben, and I'm a mixed-methods researcher based in Seattle, Washington. I have over 7 years of experience in the video game & consumer technology industries. Some titles I've worked on include Hogwarts Legacy, Hitman 2, Destiny 2, and Marathon.

I am an expert in translating complex data into actionable design insights that enhance products and inform strategic decisions. My academic background is in psychology, cognitive science, and user-centered design. I am specialized in qualitative methods (interviews, focus groups, usability testing), and quantitative methods (surveys, statistical analysis, playtesting).
